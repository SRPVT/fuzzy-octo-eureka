Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, you might find answers to common questions:

  • How do I add Editing Helper to my Discord server? - Click the "Add to Discord" button on this website and select your server. Editing Helper requires basic permissions to function properly.
  • What are the system requirements? - Editing Helper works on all Discord servers. Ensure your server has basic permissions configured correctly.
  • Is Editing Helper free? - Yes! Editing Helper is completely free with all essential features. Premium features are coming soon.
  • How do I use a command? - Type "/" in Discord to see available commands. Our bot supports Discord's slash command system.
  • Can I customize commands for my server? - Yes! Use the settings command to customize Editing Helper's behavior for your specific needs.
  • What data does Editing Helper collect? - We only collect minimal data necessary to provide the service. Please review our Privacy Policy for details.
